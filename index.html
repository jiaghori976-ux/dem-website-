Our Story

Jiya Collection was born from a love for India's rich textile heritage. Every piece in our collection is a tribute to the artisans who pour their soul into every stitch, every bead, every thread.

We work directly with master weavers and craftsmen across Varanasi, Jaipur, and Kanchipuram to bring you authentic, handcrafted luxury — designed for the modern woman who honors her roots.
